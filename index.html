Training & Workshops

I teach with a practical, human tone—so people leave confident and ready to use what they learned. I’ve worked as an instructor and teaching assistant in Data Science, Python, reproducible research, and open‑source workflows. I also organized an international conference focused on the Python scientific stack.

As a mentor, I’ve supported people across Bolivia, Brazil, the U.S., Nigeria, India and more—through internships, mentoring programs, and Google Summer of Code—helping newcomers start contributing to open source and grow their careers.

  • Mentoring & Career: 1:1 and cohorts; contribution pathways; leadership habits; compassionate feedback.

  • Programs: Internship & GSoC mentorship; contributor onboarding sprints; conference facilitation.
